  • WHAT WE DO
    We aim to engage, equip and empower people to create a sustainable habitat.
    What is MASH
    MISSION
    We are an organization committed to building a global society on the premise of sustainability, a society where sustainable living is not limited to protecting our natural environment but also includes overall human development and the social well being of a person. We will achieve this by harnessing the power of 21st century technological breakthroughs such as Mobile Applications, information dissemination through Social Media, interactive community-building events like Hackathons and an ever increasing range of other possibilities that technology has to offer.
